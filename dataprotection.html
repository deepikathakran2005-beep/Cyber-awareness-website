Data Protection & Backups

Learn best practices for protecting sensitive data and maintaining privacy

Why Data Protection Matters

Data is one of the most valuable assets in the digital age. Whether it's personal photos, financial records, business documents, or customer information, protecting this data from loss, theft, or unauthorized access is critical.

Data breaches can lead to identity theft, financial loss, reputational damage, and legal consequences.

Data Protection Best Practices

Encryption

Encrypt sensitive data both at rest and in transit using strong encryption standards.

Access Control

Implement role-based access - users should only access data necessary for their role.

Data Minimization

Only collect and retain data that's absolutely necessary for business purposes.

Secure Storage

Store sensitive data in secure, access-controlled systems with audit logs.
