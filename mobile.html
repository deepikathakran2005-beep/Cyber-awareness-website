Mobile Security

Secure your mobile devices and protect your data on the go

Why Mobile Security Matters

Mobile devices store sensitive personal and work information, making them prime targets for cybercriminals. From banking apps to email, your phone contains data that needs protection.

Mobile threats include malicious apps, unsecured WiFi, device theft, and SMS phishing (smishing).

Mobile Security Best Practices

🔒 Use Strong Device Lock

Enable a strong PIN, password, or biometric lock. Set your device to auto-lock after a short period of inactivity.

📲 Download Apps Carefully

Only install apps from official stores (Apple App Store, Google Play). Check reviews and permissions before installing.

📶 Avoid Public WiFi for Sensitive Tasks

Don't access banking or enter passwords on public WiFi. Use a VPN for secure connections when necessary.

🔄 Keep Software Updated

Enable automatic updates for your operating system and apps to get the latest security patches.

Additional Protection Measures

  • ✓Enable remote wipe capability in case of theft or loss
  • ✓Use encrypted messaging apps for sensitive communications
  • ✓Review and limit app permissions regularly
  • ✓Be cautious of SMS links and unexpected texts (smishing)
  • ✓Back up your mobile data regularly
  • ✓Disable Bluetooth when not in use

🚨 If Your Device is Lost or Stolen

Immediately report it to your carrier and employer. Use remote wipe features if available. Change passwords for all accounts accessed from that device. Monitor for suspicious activity on your accounts.
