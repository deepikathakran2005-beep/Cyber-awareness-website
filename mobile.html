Mobile Security

Protect your mobile devices from emerging security threats

Mobile Security Landscape

Mobile devices have become central to our daily lives, storing sensitive personal and business data. This makes them prime targets for cybercriminals who exploit mobile vulnerabilities through malicious apps, unsecured networks, and social engineering.

Essential Mobile Security Settings

Screen Lock & Biometrics

Enable a strong PIN, password, or biometric authentication. Set screen timeout to 30 seconds or less for automatic locking.

OS & App Updates

Keep your operating system and apps up to date with the latest security patches. Enable automatic updates in your device settings.

App Permissions

Review and limit app permissions to only what's necessary for functionality. Regularly audit app permissions and revoke unnecessary access.

Find My Device

Enable device tracking, remote lock, and remote wipe capabilities. Set up Find My iPhone (iOS) or Find My Device (Android).
