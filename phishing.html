Phishing Defense

Learn to identify and protect yourself from phishing attacks

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to trick you into revealing sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data.

These attacks typically come through email, text messages, or fake websites designed to look authentic.

How to Spot Phishing Attempts
