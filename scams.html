Scam Detection

Recognize and avoid various types of online scams and fraud attempts

Understanding Online Scams

Online scams are fraudulent schemes designed to steal money, personal information, or both. Scammers use sophisticated psychological tactics to exploit trust, fear, and urgency to manipulate victims into making poor decisions.

The key to protection is awareness. Understanding common scam types and red flags can help you spot and avoid these threats before becoming a victim.

Common Types of Scams

Investment Scams

Promises of guaranteed returns or get-rich-quick schemes that are too good to be true. No legitimate investment guarantees high returns with zero risk.

Romance Scams

Fake online relationships designed to build trust before requesting money. Be wary of online connections who quickly profess love and ask for financial help.

Tech Support Scams

Fake tech support claiming your computer has problems and requesting remote access. Legitimate companies don't cold-call about computer problems.

Prize & Lottery Scams

Notifications of winning prizes or lotteries you never entered. Real prizes don't require upfront payments or personal information.

If You've Been Scammed

Stop all communication immediately. Contact your bank, change passwords, report to authorities, document everything, and warn others who might be targeted.
